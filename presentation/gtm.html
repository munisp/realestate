Agent partnerships and developer relationships provide efficient customer acquisition channels

Agent Partnerships

Real estate agents are natural evangelists for the platform. Our partnership program includes:

  • Comprehensive training on platform features
  • Dedicated success managers for support
  • Attractive commission structures for referrals
  • Co-marketing programs and materials
  • Early access to new features

Agents bring their existing client networks, providing immediate access to qualified buyers and sellers.

Developer Relationships

Property developers seek efficient sales channels. Our developer program follows a proven expansion path:

  • Pilot projects with single developments
  • Demonstrate measurable sellout improvements
  • Expand to developer's full portfolio
  • White-label options for large developers
  • Integrated marketing and analytics

Developers bring exclusive inventory and credibility that attracts buyers to the platform.

These two partnership channels—agents and developers—provide immediate scale and credibility. Combined with our direct-to-consumer strategy, they create a comprehensive multi-channel acquisition approach with exceptional unit economics.
