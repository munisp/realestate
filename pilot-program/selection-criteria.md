# Pilot Host Selection Criteria

## Scoring System (Total: 100 points)

### 1. Property Quality (25 points)
- **Location** (10 points)
  - Lekki/VI/Ikoyi: 10 points
  - Other prime areas: 7 points
  - Other locations: 4 points

- **Property Type** (10 points)
  - 2-3 bedroom: 10 points
  - 1 bedroom: 8 points
  - Studio: 6 points
  - 4+ bedroom: 7 points

- **Nightly Rate** (5 points)
  - ₦40k-₦100k: 5 points
  - ₦25k-₦40k: 4 points
  - ₦15k-₦25k: 3 points
  - ₦100k+: 4 points

### 2. Experience & Track Record (30 points)
- **Hosting Experience** (15 points)
  - 2-5 years: 15 points
  - 1-2 years: 12 points
  - 6-12 months: 8 points
  - Less than 6 months: 5 points

- **Monthly Bookings** (10 points)
  - 21-30: 10 points
  - 11-20: 8 points
  - 6-10: 6 points
  - 0-5: 3 points

- **Platform Presence** (5 points)
  - 3+ platforms: 5 points
  - 2 platforms: 3 points
  - 1 platform: 2 points

### 3. Engagement & Commitment (25 points)
- **Pilot Commitment** (10 points)
  - Yes (5+ bookings): 10 points
  - Maybe: 5 points
  - No: 0 points

- **Onboarding Availability** (5 points)
  - Yes (in-person): 5 points
  - Yes (online): 4 points
  - No: 0 points

- **Application Quality** (10 points)
  - Detailed responses: 10 points
  - Moderate detail: 6 points
  - Minimal detail: 3 points

### 4. Strategic Value (20 points)
- **Portfolio Size** (10 points)
  - 6-10 properties: 10 points
  - 4-5 properties: 8 points
  - 2-3 properties: 6 points
  - 1 property: 4 points

- **Feature Interest Alignment** (10 points)
  - Interested in 5+ key features: 10 points
  - Interested in 3-4 features: 7 points
  - Interested in 1-2 features: 4 points

## Selection Process

### Phase 1: Initial Screening (Week 1)
- Review all applications
- Score each application
- Shortlist top 15 candidates (minimum score: 60/100)

### Phase 2: Interviews (Week 2)
- 30-minute video calls with shortlisted candidates
- Verify property details
- Assess technical readiness
- Evaluate communication skills

### Phase 3: Final Selection (Week 2)
- Select 10 hosts (8 primary + 2 backup)
- Prioritize diversity:
  - Mix of property types
  - Mix of experience levels
  - Geographic spread within target areas

### Phase 4: Onboarding (Week 3-4)
- Send welcome packages
- Schedule onboarding sessions
- Provide platform access
- Begin property setup

## Red Flags (Auto-Reject)
- Unwilling to commit to pilot terms
- No active properties
- Less than 3 months hosting experience
- Poor communication in application
- Unrealistic expectations

## Diversity Goals
- 30% new hosts (< 1 year experience)
- 40% mid-level hosts (1-3 years)
- 30% experienced hosts (3+ years)
- 50% properties in Lekki
- 30% properties in VI
- 20% properties in Ikoyi
