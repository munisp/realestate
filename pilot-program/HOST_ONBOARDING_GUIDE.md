# Host Onboarding Guide
## Welcome to the Shortlet Platform Pilot Program!

### Overview
Thank you for joining our pilot program! This guide will help you get started with listing your property and managing bookings on our platform.

---

## Getting Started

### 1. Account Setup (5 minutes)

**Step 1: Create Your Account**
- Visit the host dashboard at `https://host.yourplatform.com`
- Click "Sign Up as Host"
- Enter your details:
  - Full name
  - Email address
  - Phone number (WhatsApp-enabled)
  - Bank account details for payouts

**Step 2: Verify Your Identity**
- Upload a valid government-issued ID
- Take a selfie for verification
- Verification typically takes 24-48 hours

**Step 3: Set Up Payment**
- Link your bank account or mobile money wallet
- Choose payout frequency (weekly or monthly)
- Payouts are 97% of booking amount (3% platform fee)

---

### 2. List Your Property (15 minutes)

**Property Details**
1. Navigate to "Properties" → "Add New Property"
2. Fill in basic information:
   - Property title (e.g., "Luxury 2BR in Lekki Phase 1")
   - Full address
   - Property type (apartment, house, studio)
   - Number of bedrooms, bathrooms
   - Maximum guests

**Photos**
- Upload at least 10 high-quality photos
- Include:
  - Living room
  - All bedrooms
  - Kitchen
  - Bathrooms
  - Exterior/building
  - Amenities (pool, gym, etc.)
- Photos should be well-lit and show the space clearly

**Amenities**
Select all amenities available:
- WiFi
- Air conditioning
- TV
- Kitchen
- Washing machine
- Parking
- Pool
- Gym
- Security
- Generator/backup power

**Pricing**
- Set your base nightly rate in Naira (₦)
- Set cleaning fee (one-time charge)
- Platform will automatically apply:
  - 30% weekend premium (Friday-Sunday)
  - 10% discount for stays 7+ nights
  - 15% discount for stays 14+ nights

**House Rules**
- Check-in time (default: 2 PM)
- Check-out time (default: 11 AM)
- Smoking policy
- Pets policy
- Party/events policy
- Additional rules

---

### 3. Calendar & Availability

**Sync with Airbnb/Booking.com**
1. Go to "Settings" → "Channel Manager"
2. Click "Connect" next to Airbnb
3. Copy your Airbnb iCal URL
4. Paste and save
5. Calendar syncs every hour automatically

**Manual Blocking**
- Go to "Calendar"
- Click dates you want to block
- Add reason (personal use, maintenance, etc.)

---

### 4. Managing Bookings

**New Booking Request**
You'll receive:
- WhatsApp notification
- Email notification
- Dashboard notification

**Actions:**
1. Review guest profile
2. Click "Confirm" or "Decline"
3. If confirmed, guest receives:
   - Booking confirmation via WhatsApp
   - Payment instructions
   - Property details

**Check-in Day**
- Guest receives automated check-in instructions 24 hours before
- Includes:
  - Property address
  - Access code/key pickup details
  - WiFi password
  - House rules reminder
  - Your contact number

**During Stay**
- Guests can message you via WhatsApp
- All messages appear in dashboard "Messages" tab
- Respond within 2 hours for best guest experience

**Check-out**
- Guest checks out by 11 AM
- You inspect property
- Click "Check Out" in dashboard
- Guest receives review request via WhatsApp
- Payout processes within 24 hours

### 6. Guest Communication

**Best Practices:**
- Respond within 2 hours
- Be professional and friendly
- Use WhatsApp for quick questions
- Provide clear, detailed answers
- Set expectations upfront

**Common Guest Questions:**
- "Is parking available?" → Specify if free or paid
- "How far from airport?" → Provide exact distance and transport options
- "Is there backup power?" → Specify generator capacity and hours
- "Can I check in early?" → State your policy clearly
