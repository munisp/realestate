# Pilot Program Terms & Conditions
## Lagos Shortlet Platform - Host Pilot Program

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025  
**Program Duration:** 3 months (renewable)

---

### 1. Program Overview

This Pilot Program ("Program") is offered by [Your Company Name] ("Platform", "we", "us") to selected shortlet property hosts ("Host", "you") in Lagos, Nigeria.

**Purpose:** To test and refine our shortlet booking platform before public launch.

**Benefits:**
- 0% platform commission for 3 months (normally 3%)
- Free professional photography session
- Priority customer support
- Featured listing placement
- Early access to new features

---

### 2. Eligibility

**To participate, you must:**
- Own or have legal authority to rent properties in Lagos
- Provide valid identification and bank account details
- Maintain active WhatsApp number for communications
- Commit to providing monthly feedback
- Maintain minimum 90% response rate
- Achieve minimum 4.5/5.0 guest rating

---

### 3. Host Obligations

**You agree to:**
- List accurate property information and photos
- Respond to booking inquiries within 2 hours
- Honor all confirmed bookings
- Maintain property to advertised standards
- Provide safe, clean accommodation
- Comply with local laws and regulations
- Participate in feedback sessions (minimum 2)
- Submit monthly feedback surveys

---

### 4. Platform Fees

**During Pilot (Months 1-3):**
- Platform commission: 0%
- Payment processing: Included
- You receive: 100% of booking amount

**After Pilot:**
- Platform commission: 3% of booking amount
- Payment processing: Included
- You receive: 97% of booking amount

**Example:**
- Guest pays: ₦50,000
- Platform fee (3%): ₦1,500
- You receive: ₦48,500

---

### 5. Payments & Payouts

**Payment Processing:**
- Guests pay via Paystack or Flutterwave
- Funds held in escrow until checkout
- Automatic payout 24 hours after checkout

**Payout Schedule:**
- Standard: 24 hours after checkout
- Bank transfer or mobile money
- Minimum payout: ₦5,000

**Cancellations:**
- Guest cancels >48 hours before check-in: Full refund
- Guest cancels <48 hours: 50% refund, 50% to host
- Host cancels: Full refund + ₦10,000 penalty

### 6. Booking Management

**Acceptance:**
- Respond to booking requests within 24 hours
- Confirm or decline with reason
- Instant booking option available

**Calendar:**
- Keep availability updated
- Sync with Airbnb/Booking.com (optional)
- Block personal use dates

**Check-in/Check-out:**
- Provide clear access instructions
- Verify guest identity
- Inspect property after checkout

### 7. Guest Communication
