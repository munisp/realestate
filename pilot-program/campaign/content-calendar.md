### Common Questions & Answers

**Q: Is there really 0% commission?**
A: Yes! During the 3-month pilot (Feb-Apr 2025), you keep 100% of your earnings. After pilot, our standard commission is just 3%.

**Q: What if I'm already on Airbnb?**
A: Perfect! Our calendar sync works with Airbnb, Booking.com, and other platforms. No double bookings.

**Q: How fast are payouts?**
A: Within 24 hours of guest checkout via Paystack or Flutterwave. You choose your preferred method.

**Q: What's required for the pilot?**
A: Minimum 1 active property in Lekki/VI/Ikoyi, commitment to 5 bookings over 3 months, and willingness to provide feedback.

**Q: When does the pilot start?**
A: February 1, 2025. Onboarding sessions start in late January.
