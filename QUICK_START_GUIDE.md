## Getting Started

The Enterprise Real Estate Platform is accessible through three channels: our web application, mobile apps for iOS and Android, and an admin dashboard for property managers and agents. Most users will start with the web application, which provides the complete feature set in an intuitive interface.

### Creating Your Account

Visit **https://realestate.com** and click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner. You will be prompted to provide basic information including your name, email address, and password. Choose a strong password with at least eight characters, including uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.

After submitting the form, check your email for a verification link. Click this link to activate your account. Email verification ensures the security of your account and enables important notifications about properties and transactions.

### Choosing Your Role

During signup, you will select your primary role on the platform. **Buyers** are searching for properties to purchase or rent. **Sellers** are listing properties for sale. **Agents** represent buyers or sellers and manage multiple listings. **Developers** manage construction projects and unit inventory. **Property Managers** oversee rental properties and tenant relationships.

Your role determines which features are most prominent in your interface, but you can always access other features as needed. Roles can be updated later in your account settings.

## Your First Login

After creating your account and verifying your email, return to **https://realestate.com** and click "Log In". Enter your email and password, then click "Sign In". You will be taken to your personalized dashboard.

### Understanding the Dashboard

The dashboard provides an at-a-glance view of your activity on the platform. For buyers, this includes saved searches, favorite properties, recent views, and recommendations based on your preferences. For agents, the dashboard shows active listings, recent inquiries, upcoming appointments, and performance metrics.

The navigation bar at the top provides quick access to key features. **Properties** takes you to the search interface. **Map View** displays properties geographically. **Dashboard** returns you to this overview. **Favorites** shows properties you have saved. Your profile icon in the top right provides access to account settings, notifications, and logout.

### Completing Your Profile

Click your profile icon and select "Settings" to complete your profile. Adding information such as your phone number, location, and property preferences helps the platform provide better recommendations and enables agents to contact you about relevant listings.

For buyers, specify your budget range, preferred locations, property types, and must-have features. This information powers our recommendation engine and helps filter search results to show only properties that match your criteria.

## Exploring Properties

The heart of the platform is property discovery. Click "Properties" in the navigation bar to access the main search interface. By default, you will see featured properties curated by our team, but you can immediately begin searching for properties that match your specific needs.

### Property Cards

Each property is displayed as a card showing the primary image, price, address, key details (bedrooms, bathrooms, square footage), and a brief description. Hover over a card to see additional images in a quick preview. Click anywhere on the card to view the full property details.

### Property Details

The property detail page provides comprehensive information about a specific listing. High-resolution images are displayed in a gallery at the top, with navigation arrows to browse through all available photos. Below the gallery, you will find the property description, detailed specifications, amenities, and location information.

A map shows the exact property location and nearby points of interest including schools, parks, shopping, and public transportation. The **Neighborhood** section provides demographic information, crime statistics, and school ratings to help you evaluate the area.

For properties with virtual tours or 3D models, an **Explore in AR** button allows you to visualize the property using augmented reality on compatible devices. This feature brings properties to life and helps you understand the space better than photos alone.

### Contacting the Agent

Every property listing includes information about the listing agent. Click "Contact Agent" to send a message directly through the platform. Your message is delivered instantly, and you will receive a notification when the agent responds. All communication is logged, providing a complete history of your interactions.

## Advanced Search

While browsing featured properties is a great way to explore, the advanced search interface helps you find exactly what you are looking for. Click the search icon or press the "/" key to open the search panel.

### Search Filters

The search interface provides dozens of filters to narrow results. Start by specifying your **location** using city, neighborhood, ZIP code, or even drawing a custom area on the map. Set your **price range** using the slider or by entering specific minimum and maximum values.

Filter by **property type** including single-family homes, condos, townhouses, multi-family properties, and land. Specify the number of **bedrooms** and **bathrooms** you need. Set minimum and maximum **square footage** to find properties of the right size.

Advanced filters include **year built**, **lot size**, **garage spaces**, **stories**, and specific **amenities** such as pools, fireplaces, hardwood floors, or smart home features. You can even filter by **school district** or proximity to specific addresses.

### Geospatial Search

The map view provides powerful geospatial search capabilities. Click "Map View" in the navigation to see all properties displayed on an interactive map. Zoom and pan to explore different areas. Click any marker to see a preview of that property.

Use the **Draw** tool to define a custom search area. Click points on the map to create a polygon, and the platform will show only properties within that boundary. This is perfect for searching near specific locations like your workplace, a school, or family members.

The **Radius Search** feature finds properties within a specified distance of a point. Enter an address and radius (in miles or kilometers), and the platform displays all matching properties. This is ideal for commute-based searches.

### Saving Searches

Once you have configured filters to match your criteria, click "Save Search" to preserve these settings. Saved searches can be named and accessed quickly from your dashboard. Even better, enable **notifications** for a saved search to receive alerts when new properties matching your criteria are listed.

Notifications can be delivered via email, push notification on mobile devices, or in-app alerts. Configure the frequency to receive updates immediately, daily, or weekly based on your preferences.

## Saving Favorites

As you browse properties, you will find listings you want to remember. Click the heart icon on any property card or detail page to add it to your favorites. Favorited properties are accessible from the "Favorites" section in the navigation bar.

### Organizing Favorites

The favorites section allows you to organize properties into custom lists. Create lists for different purposes such as "Top Choices", "Backup Options", or "Investment Properties". Drag and drop properties between lists to reorganize them.

Each list can be shared with others via a unique link. This is perfect for collaborating with a spouse, family member, or agent. Recipients can view the properties but cannot modify your lists unless you grant them permission.

### Comparing Properties

Select multiple properties from your favorites and click "Compare" to see them side-by-side. The comparison view displays key specifications, pricing, and features in a table format, making it easy to evaluate trade-offs and make informed decisions.

## Making an Offer

When you find a property you want to purchase, the platform streamlines the offer process. From the property detail page, click "Make an Offer" to begin.

### Offer Details

Enter your offer price, proposed closing date, and any contingencies such as financing or inspection. You can attach documents such as pre-approval letters directly through the platform. The offer form guides you through all required information, ensuring nothing is missed.

If you are working with an agent, they will receive a notification and can review your offer before submission. Agents can suggest modifications or approve the offer as-is. Once approved, the offer is transmitted to the listing agent instantly.

### Negotiation

The platform facilitates the negotiation process. When the seller responds with a counteroffer, you receive a notification with the details. You can accept the counteroffer, reject it, or submit a new counteroffer. All offers and counteroffers are logged, providing a complete negotiation history.

Once both parties agree on terms, the platform generates the purchase agreement and coordinates signatures through integrated e-signature capabilities. All documents are stored securely and accessible at any time.

### Transaction Tracking

After an offer is accepted, the transaction moves to the closing phase. The platform provides a timeline showing all required steps including inspection, appraisal, financing, and final walkthrough. Each milestone is tracked, and you receive reminders as deadlines approach.

Upload documents such as inspection reports, appraisal results, and loan documents directly to the transaction. All parties including buyers, sellers, agents, and attorneys have access to relevant documents, ensuring transparency and reducing delays.

## Mobile App

The Enterprise Real Estate Platform mobile app brings the full power of the platform to your iOS or Android device. Download the app from the App Store or Google Play by searching for "Enterprise Real Estate Platform".

### Mobile Features

The mobile app provides all core features including property search, favorites, messaging with agents, and transaction tracking. The interface is optimized for mobile devices with large touch targets, swipe gestures, and streamlined navigation.

**Push notifications** keep you informed of new listings, price changes, agent messages, and transaction updates. Configure notification preferences in the app settings to control what alerts you receive.

**Biometric authentication** using Face ID or Touch ID provides secure, convenient access without entering your password each time. Enable this feature in settings after your first login.

### Augmented Reality

One of the most exciting mobile features is **augmented reality property visualization**. When viewing a property with 3D models, tap "View in AR" to see the property overlaid on your surroundings. Point your camera at an empty space, and the property appears at full scale.

Walk around the AR model to explore from different angles. This feature helps you understand the size and layout of properties in a way that photos cannot convey. It is particularly useful for new construction or properties you cannot visit in person.

### Offline Mode

The mobile app supports offline access to your favorites and recently viewed properties. When you lose internet connectivity, you can still browse these properties, view images, and read descriptions. Any actions you take (such as favoriting additional properties) are queued and synchronized when connectivity is restored.

## For Agents

Agents have access to additional features designed to streamline listing management, lead tracking, and client communication. After logging in, click "Agent Dashboard" to access these tools.

### Creating Listings

Click "New Listing" to create a property listing. The listing form guides you through all required information including address, price, property details, description, and photos. Upload multiple photos, and the platform automatically optimizes them for fast loading while preserving quality.

Advanced listing features include **virtual tours**, **3D models**, **floor plans**, and **video walkthroughs**. These rich media assets significantly increase engagement and help properties stand out.

Once a listing is created, it is immediately searchable by buyers. The platform automatically generates SEO-optimized pages for each listing, improving visibility in search engines.

### Lead Management

The CRM section provides comprehensive lead management. All inquiries about your listings are captured as leads with complete contact information and inquiry details. Leads are automatically assigned to you and appear in your dashboard.

Track the status of each lead through customizable stages such as "New", "Contacted", "Showing Scheduled", "Offer Made", and "Closed". Set reminders to follow up with leads at appropriate intervals. All communication with leads is logged, providing context for every interaction.

### Activity Tracking

Log all interactions with clients including phone calls, emails, meetings, and property showings. The activity log provides a complete history of your relationship with each client, making it easy to pick up where you left off.

Set tasks and reminders for future actions. The dashboard shows upcoming tasks and overdue items, ensuring nothing falls through the cracks.

### Performance Analytics

The analytics section provides insights into your performance. Track metrics such as listings views, inquiries generated, offers received, and deals closed. Compare your performance across time periods to identify trends.

Property-level analytics show which listings are performing well and which may need price adjustments or better marketing. View metrics such as views, favorites, and time on market.

## For Developers

Property developers have access to specialized tools for managing construction projects, tracking unit inventory, and analyzing sales performance. Access these features from the "Developer Portal" after logging in.

### Project Management

Create projects representing your developments. For each project, specify details such as location, total units, construction timeline, and amenities. Upload site plans, renderings, and marketing materials.

Track construction milestones and update the project status as work progresses. Buyers can see the current construction phase and estimated completion date, setting appropriate expectations.

### Unit Inventory

For each project, define individual units with specific characteristics such as unit number, floor, square footage, bedrooms, bathrooms, and price. Specify the status of each unit as "Available", "Reserved", "Under Contract", or "Sold".

The inventory view provides an at-a-glance overview of sales progress. Filter and sort units by various criteria to quickly find specific inventory. Export inventory data to spreadsheets for offline analysis.

### Sales Tracking

Monitor sales performance with detailed analytics. View metrics such as units sold, revenue generated, average price per square foot, and sales velocity. Compare performance across different unit types or floors to inform pricing strategies.

The sales funnel shows how many buyers have expressed interest, scheduled visits, made offers, and completed purchases. Identify bottlenecks in the sales process and take corrective action.

## Tips for Success

As you begin using the platform, keep these tips in mind to get the most value:

**Complete your profile** fully to receive better recommendations and enable agents to contact you effectively. The more information you provide, the more personalized your experience becomes.

**Set up saved searches** for your key criteria and enable notifications. This ensures you are among the first to know when relevant properties are listed, giving you a competitive advantage in hot markets.

**Use the mobile app** to stay connected on the go. Enable push notifications so you never miss important updates about properties or transactions.

**Communicate through the platform** rather than external email or phone when possible. This ensures all communication is logged and accessible to relevant parties, reducing misunderstandings and providing an audit trail.

**Explore all features** even if they do not seem immediately relevant. The platform is rich with capabilities that can save time and provide insights you might not have considered.

**Provide feedback** about your experience. We continuously improve the platform based on user input. Use the feedback form accessible from any page to share suggestions or report issues.
