# Pilot Recruitment Campaign Launch Guide

Complete guide for launching the Lagos pilot recruitment campaign, processing applications, and onboarding the first 10 hosts.

## Campaign Overview

The pilot recruitment campaign targets **10 high-quality hosts** in premium Lagos neighborhoods (Lekki, Victoria Island, Ikoyi) to validate the platform's value proposition and operational workflows before full market launch. The campaign runs for **2 weeks** with a structured application, screening, and onboarding process.

### Campaign Objectives

| Objective | Target | Success Metric |
|-----------|--------|----------------|
| Total Applications | 50+ | Application volume |
| Qualified Applicants | 25+ | Pass initial screening |
| Onboarded Hosts | 10 | Complete onboarding |
| First Listing Published | 8+ | Active listings |
| First Booking | 5+ | Booking conversion |

### Timeline

| Phase | Duration | Activities |
|-------|----------|------------|
| **Pre-Launch** | 3 days | Finalize materials, set up tracking, brief team |
| **Campaign Launch** | Week 1 | Activate all channels, accept applications |
| **Screening** | Week 2 | Review applications, conduct interviews |
| **Onboarding** | Week 3 | Train hosts, create listings, prepare properties |
| **Soft Launch** | Week 4 | First bookings, support and monitoring |

## Week 2: Screening and Interviews

### Application Scoring System

Score each application on a 100-point scale across five criteria:

| Criterion | Weight | Scoring Guide |
|-----------|--------|---------------|
| **Location** | 30 points | Lekki/VI/Ikoyi (30), Ajah/Ikeja (20), Other (10) |
| **Property Quality** | 25 points | 3-4 BR, modern (25), 2 BR (20), 1 BR (10) |
| **Hosting Experience** | 15 points | Prior hosting (15), Property management (10), None (5) |
| **Tech Proficiency** | 15 points | Self-rated 4-5 (15), 3 (10), 1-2 (5) |
| **Responsiveness** | 15 points | <24hr response (15), <48hr (10), >48hr (5) |

**Scoring Examples**:

```
Applicant A:
- Location: Lekki Phase 1 (30 points)
- Property: 3BR, furnished, modern (25 points)
- Experience: Managed Airbnb for 2 years (15 points)
- Tech: Self-rated 5/5 (15 points)
- Response: Applied within 2 hours of launch (15 points)
Total: 100 points → Top priority

Applicant B:
- Location: Ikeja GRA (20 points)
- Property: 2BR, basic furnishing (20 points)
- Experience: None (5 points)
- Tech: Self-rated 3/5 (10 points)
- Response: Applied 36 hours after launch (10 points)
Total: 65 points → Medium priority
```

**Shortlisting Criteria**:
- **Top 15** (Score ≥80): Immediate interview invitation
- **Next 10** (Score 65-79): Waitlist, interview if top candidates decline
- **Bottom** (Score <65): Polite rejection, offer to join waitlist for full launch

### Interview Process

**Interview Format**: 30-minute video call (Google Meet or Zoom)

**Interview Script**:

```
Introduction (3 minutes):
"Hi [Name], thanks for joining! I'm [Your Name] from [Company]. 
This call will take about 30 minutes. I'll ask about your property, 
hosting goals, and answer any questions you have. Sound good?"

Property Details (8 minutes):
1. "Tell me about your property. What makes it special?"
2. "How often is it available for short-term rentals?"
3. "What's your ideal guest profile?"
4. "Have you hosted before? What was your experience?"

Platform Fit (7 minutes):
5. "What attracted you to our platform?"
6. "How comfortable are you with technology? (booking systems, apps, etc.)"
7. "How would you handle a guest issue at 11 PM?"
8. "What are your earnings expectations?"

Logistics (7 minutes):
9. "Can you commit to responding to booking inquiries within 4 hours?"
10. "Are you willing to provide ID verification for your guests?"
11. "Can you attend a 2-hour onboarding training next week?"

Q&A (5 minutes):
"What questions do you have for me?"

Closing:
"Thanks for your time! We'll make a decision within 48 hours and 
follow up via email. Any final questions?"
```

**Red Flags** (Immediate disqualification):
- Property not actually owned by applicant
- Unrealistic earnings expectations (>₦1M/month for 2BR)
- Unwilling to verify guest IDs
- Poor communication or unprofessional behavior
- Property in unsafe or undesirable location

**Green Flags** (Strong positive indicators):
- Prior hosting experience with good reviews
- Professional, responsive communication
- Realistic expectations and business mindset
- High-quality property in prime location
- Willing to provide feedback and collaborate

### Interview Scoring

Rate each candidate on a 5-point scale across four dimensions:

| Dimension | 1 (Poor) | 3 (Good) | 5 (Excellent) |
|-----------|----------|----------|---------------|
| **Property Quality** | Below standard | Meets requirements | Exceptional |
| **Communication** | Unclear, slow | Clear, timely | Professional, proactive |
| **Tech Savvy** | Struggles with basics | Comfortable | Advanced user |
| **Cultural Fit** | Misaligned values | Good fit | Perfect match |

**Selection Criteria**:
- **Approve**: Total score ≥16 AND no red flags
- **Waitlist**: Total score 12-15
- **Reject**: Total score <12 OR any red flags

**Target**: Approve 12-15 candidates (to account for 2-3 dropouts)
